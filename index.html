Our History

Bortoli Wellington Pty Ltd. was incorporated in 1995 to provide professional land surveying services to organisations and individuals. Our services are continually monitored and updated to current industry standards to enhance client outcomes.

Our Directors

The directors, Robert Bortoli and Scott Wellington, together possess 50+ years of experience across all facets of the profession. We are a member firm of the Association of Consulting Surveyors, and the directors are members of the Institution of Surveyors, Victoria.
