About Us

Bortoli Wellington Pty Ltd. was incorporated in 1995 to provide professional land surveying services to organisations and individuals. Our services are continually monitored and updated to current industry standards to enhance the outcome for our clients.

The directors, Robert Bortoli and Scott Wellington, together possess 50+ years of experience covering all facets of the profession. We are a member firm of the Association of Consulting Surveyors, and the directors are members of the Institution of Surveyors, Victoria.

All projects are directly supervised by one or both directors and delivered by a team of qualified surveyors, experienced survey technicians and assistants, and CAD draftspersons.
